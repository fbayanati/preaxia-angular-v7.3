Open your PreAxia HSA

Sole Proprietorship For self-employed and unincorporated businesses.

Choose this option if you are self-employed and your business is not incorporated. Managing your HSA is easy and you can submit claims for you or on behalf of your dependents (for example family members).

Incorporated Business For incorporated businesses with or without employees.

Choose this option if you have an incorporated business. You will be able to manage multiple HSA plans with different contribution limits for your employees. Your employees will be able to sign-in to manage their HSAs and submit claims.
